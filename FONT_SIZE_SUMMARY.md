# Font Size Summary - Client Portal

## Current Font Size Usage Analysis

### Headers (H1/H2/H3 - Large Titles)
- **56px** - Used 3 times (Tier benefit numbers in Activity page)
- **52px** - Used 2 times (Hero banners: Account, Projects pages)
- **44px** - Used 1 time (FAQ hero banner)
- **42px** - Used 2 times (Contact hero banner, Overview hero title mobile)
- **40px** - Used 1 time (Account page section title)
- **36px** - Used 3 times (Tier benefit titles, Overview hero title desktop)
- **34px** - Used 2 times (Proposal titles in Overview)
- **32px** - Used 3 times (FAQ section titles, Profile section title mobile)
- **24px** - Used 3 times (Various section titles)
- **22px** - Used 1 time (Contact page team member names)
- **20px** - Used 12 times (Section headers: Tier Benefits, Contributing Projects, etc.)

### Subheaders & Medium Text
- **18px** - Used 6 times (Subsection titles, Overview signature)
- **16px** - Used 13 times (Body text emphasis, Overview subtitle, Loading text)
- **15px** - Used 16 times (Body text, Modal buttons, Form labels)
- **14px** - Used 65 times ⭐ **MOST COMMON** (Primary body text, buttons, form inputs, table cells)
- **13px** - Used 30 times (Secondary body text, FAQ answers, smaller labels)

### Small Text & Labels
- **12px** - Used 52 times ⭐ **SECOND MOST COMMON** (Navigation, small labels, table headers, buttons)
- **11px** - Used 47 times (Tiny labels, captions, FAQ navigation)
- **10px** - Used 6 times (Very small labels, timestamps)
- **9px** - Used 9 times (Smallest text, footnotes)

### Special Cases
- **120px** - Used 1 time (Contact page initials background)
- **80px** - Used 1 time (Contact page initials fallback)

## Inconsistencies Identified

### Hero Banner Titles
- Account: 52px
- Projects: 52px  
- FAQ: 44px ⚠️ **INCONSISTENT**
- Contact: 42px ⚠️ **INCONSISTENT**

### Section Headers (H2/H3)
- Most section headers: 20px
- FAQ section titles: 32px ⚠️ **INCONSISTENT**
- Profile title: 20px
- Some subsection titles: 18px

### Body Text
- Primary body: 14px (most common)
- Secondary body: 13px
- Some body text: 15px or 16px ⚠️ **INCONSISTENT**

### Navigation & Labels
- Navigation links: 12px
- Small labels: 11px or 12px ⚠️ **SOMEWHAT INCONSISTENT**

## Recommended Standardization

### Headers
- **Hero Banner Titles**: 44px (standardize all to match FAQ)
- **Page Section Headers (H2)**: 20px (already mostly consistent)
- **Subsection Headers (H3)**: 18px

### Body Text
- **Primary Body**: 14px (keep as standard)
- **Secondary Body**: 13px (for less important text)
- **Emphasized Body**: 15px (for important callouts)

### UI Elements
- **Navigation Links**: 12px
- **Buttons**: 14px
- **Form Labels**: 13px
- **Small Labels/Captions**: 11px
- **Tiny Text**: 10px

### Special
- **Large Numbers**: 36px or 56px (for tier benefits)
- **Initials Background**: 120px (decorative only)
